Payout Process

As an Independent Contractor, you are growing your Company through recruitment. Each member that becomes part of your network is your Downline and you receive a percentage of their gross earnings. However, to be eligible to receive the commissions from your Downline you must provide the Rideshare service for a required number of parttime hours each week. Yet, as an Independent Contractor with Rideshare, you will always receive 80% of the gross fare.

Rideshare Compensation Plan

  • Rideshare and/or Independent Contractor/ Driver (a.k.a. Company in below illustration) recruits Driver 1.
  • Driver 1 works one day and earns $100 as a gross amount.
  • Rideshare deducts 15.5% and Driver receives 4.5% as commission from the 1st tier downline.
  • Driver 1 invites Driver 1a to become a member of the platform.
  • Driver 1a works one day and earns $100 as a gross amount.
  • Driver 1 receives 4.5% as a commission from their 1st tier, Driver 1a.
  • Rideshare deducts 12.5% and Driver receives 3% as commission from the 2nd tier, Driver 1a.
  • Driver 1a invites Driver 1b to become a member of the platform.
  • Driver 1b works for one day and earns $100 as a gross amount.
  • Driver 1a receives 4.5% as commission from their 1st tier, Driver 1b.
  • Driver 1 receives 3% as commission from their 2nd tier, Driver 1b.
  • Rideshare deducts 10.75% and Driver receives 1.75% as commission from the 3rd tier, Driver 1b.
  • Driver 1b invites Driver 1c to become a member of the platform.
  • Driver 1c works for one day and earns $100 as a gross amount.
  • Driver 1b receives 4.5% as commission from their 1st tier, Driver 1c.
  • Driver 1a receives 3% as commission from their 2nd tier, Driver 1c.
  • Driver 1 receives 1.75% as commission from their 3rd tier, Driver 1c.
  • Rideshare deducts 10% and Driver receives 0.75% from the 4th tier, Driver 1c.

Financial Breakdown:

$100 earned – 20% deduction = $80 net amount

The 20% deduction breakdown:

10% = tiered payout

10% = Rideshare deduction

Upline commissions for Driver (a.k.a. Company) from Downline:

1st tier: $100 – 4.5% = $4.50

2nd tier: $100 – 3% = $3.00

3rd tier: $100 – 1.75% = $1.75

4th tier: $100 – 0.75% = $0.75

*In this example, if one person from each tier makes $100, then the Original Member (a.k.a. Company) as the Upline receives $10 as a commission from their Downline. If the commission eligibility requirement is not met during the work week, then the commission is passed to Rideshare.

Definitions:

Upline – The person or business entity that is recruiting and establishing a network is the Upline and receives percentage earnings from their Downline.

Downline – The people that have been recruited are the Downline and pay a percentage of their earnings to the Upline members.
